### Week 1 Plan – 1 Hour Per Day (Completely Free)

**Goal by end of Week 1**: Build strong foundation in Responsible AI + make good progress on Security+ SY0-701.

#### Day 1 (60 minutes)
- Read and study **Google Introduction to Responsible AI** (free guide):  
  https://developers.google.com/machine-learning/guides/intro-responsible-ai
- Write 3-sentence “Why + Innovation” reflection in your notes.

#### Day 2 (60 minutes)
- Finish any remaining parts of Google Introduction to Responsible AI.
- Professor Messer Security+ SY0-701: Watch Videos **1.1 + 1.2**.
- Practice: Professor Messer Domain 1 pop quiz (5–10 questions).
- Write short “Why + Innovation” reflection.

#### Day 3 (60 minutes)
- Start **Microsoft Learn: Embrace Responsible AI Principles** (free).  
  Complete the first 2 modules.
- Professor Messer: Watch Videos **1.3 + 1.4**.
- Practice: 10 free questions from ExamCompass or Professor Messer.

#### Day 4 (60 minutes)
- Continue Microsoft Learn Responsible AI modules.
- Professor Messer: Watch Videos **1.5 + 1.6**.
- Real-world practice: Search “recent AI governance failure 2026” and read one short article.
- Write 1 paragraph note: “What governance was missing?”

#### Day 5 (60 minutes)
- Finish Microsoft Learn Responsible AI path (as much as available).
- Professor Messer: Watch Videos **2.1 + 2.2**.
- Practice: 10 free questions (Professor Messer or ITExams).
- Write short reflection.

#### Day 6 (60 minutes)
- Start **Anthropic AI Fluency: Framework & Foundations** (free on Anthropic Academy).  
  Complete Module 1 + Module 2.
- Professor Messer: Watch Video **2.3**.
- Real-world practice: Read one recent AI security news article.
- Write short note on key ethical points.

#### Day 7 (Sunday – 60 minutes)
- Weekly review: Go over everything learned this week.
- Full “Why + Innovation” reflection for the entire week.
- Sunday Adaptability Radar: Check OWASP GenAI page + NIST AI RMF → write 3 sentences in your notes.
- Job exposure: Read 3 junior “AI Governance Analyst” or “Responsible AI Security” job postings.
